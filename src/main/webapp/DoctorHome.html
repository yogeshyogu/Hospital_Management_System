They on this time are not with their families, suffering a lot of pain but still for them thier patients are everything. They can't see our sadness. That mask which we want to get rid off they wear it mostly and treat their patients and for their happiness do a lot of things for us. Yes they are strangers too but we don't maintain distance for them instead we go and talk to them when we are sick. we feel them everything. so yes they are not god but not less than god too
